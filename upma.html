Upma

A light and savory South Indian breakfast dish made with roasted semolina (rava) and sautéed vegetables. Simple, comforting, and quick to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Semolina (Rava/Sooji)
  • 2 cups Water
  • 1 tbsp Oil or Ghee
  • 1/2 tsp Mustard Seeds
  • 1/2 tsp Urad Dal
  • 1 small Onion (chopped)
  • 1 Green Chili (chopped)
  • 5–6 Curry Leaves
  • 1/4 cup Chopped Vegetables (carrot, peas, beans - optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh Coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Dry roast the semolina in a pan until light golden. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil/ghee in a pan. Add mustard seeds and urad dal. Let them splutter.
  3. Add curry leaves, green chili, and chopped onions. Sauté until translucent.
  4. Add optional vegetables and sauté for 2–3 minutes.
  5. Pour in water and bring to a boil. Add salt.
  6. Slowly add roasted semolina, stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  7. Cook on low flame until the upma thickens and the water is absorbed.
  8. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.
