Fish Curry

A flavorful and aromatic Indian fish curry made with coastal spices, tangy tamarind, and creamy coconut milk. Best enjoyed with steamed rice or parathas.

Ingredients

  • 500g Fish pieces (Rohu, Pomfret, or any firm fish)
  • 1 Onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 Tomatoes (chopped or pureed)
  • 1 tsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1/2 cup Coconut Milk (optional)
  • 1 tbsp Tamarind pulp or 1 Tomato (for tanginess)
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh Coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Clean and marinate the fish with salt and turmeric. Set aside for 15 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and fry the marinated fish lightly. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, sauté onions until golden. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook well.
  4. Add tomatoes, tamarind (or extra tomato), and all dry spices. Cook until oil separates.
  5. Add coconut milk and bring to a simmer.
  6. Gently slide in the fish pieces and cook on low heat for 10–12 minutes.
  7. Garnish with coriander and serve hot with rice or roti.
