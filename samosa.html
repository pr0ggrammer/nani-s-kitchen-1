Samosa

A crispy and flaky deep-fried pastry filled with a spicy potato and peas mixture. A popular Indian snack enjoyed with chutneys and tea.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups All-purpose Flour (Maida)
  • 2 tbsp Oil or Ghee (for dough)
  • 1/2 tsp Carom Seeds (Ajwain)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed to knead dough)
  • 2-3 Boiled Potatoes (mashed)
  • 1/2 cup Green Peas (boiled)
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1 tsp Ginger (grated)
  • 1-2 Green Chilies (chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1/2 tsp Amchur (Dry Mango Powder)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil (for deep frying)

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, ajwain, salt, and oil. Add water gradually to form a stiff dough. Rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat a little oil, add cumin, ginger, chilies. Sauté. Add mashed potatoes, peas, spices, and mix well. Let it cool.
  3. Divide dough into small balls, roll into ovals, cut in half to form a cone.
  4. Fill the cone with stuffing and seal edges with water.
  5. Heat oil on medium. Deep fry samosas until golden and crisp.
  6. Serve hot with mint or tamarind chutney.
