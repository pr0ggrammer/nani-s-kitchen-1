Biryani

A fragrant and flavorful rice dish made with layers of aromatic basmati rice, spiced vegetables or meat, and herbs. A celebration on a plate!

📝 Ingredients

  • 1 cup Basmati Rice
  • 200g Chicken or Vegetables
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 1 large Onion (fried)
  • 1 tsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Biryani Masala
  • 1/4 tsp Saffron soaked in milk
  • 2 tbsp Ghee or Oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh Mint and Coriander leaves

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Soak basmati rice for 30 minutes and cook till 80% done. Drain and set aside.
  2. Marinate chicken/veggies with yogurt, spices, and ginger-garlic paste for 30 minutes.
  3. Heat ghee in a pan, add marinated mixture and cook till tender.
  4. In a deep pot, layer cooked chicken/veg, fried onions, rice, mint, coriander, and saffron milk.
  5. Cover and cook on low flame (dum) for 20 minutes.
  6. Fluff gently and serve hot with raita or salan.
