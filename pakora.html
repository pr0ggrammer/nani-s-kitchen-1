Pakora

Crispy, deep-fried fritters made with vegetables or paneer dipped in a spiced gram flour batter. Perfect as a tea-time snack, especially during the monsoon.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Besan (Gram Flour)
  • 1 Onion (sliced)
  • 1 Potato (thinly sliced or grated)
  • 1/2 cup Spinach or Cabbage (optional)
  • 1-2 Green Chilies (chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1/2 tsp Carom Seeds (Ajwain)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed for batter)
  • Oil (for deep frying)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix all vegetables with gram flour and spices.
  2. Gradually add water to make a thick coating batter.
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan on medium flame.
  4. Drop spoonfuls or hand-formed bits of the mixture into hot oil.
  5. Fry until golden and crispy, turning occasionally.
  6. Drain on paper towels and serve hot with green chutney or ketchup.
