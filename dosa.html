Dosa

A crispy, golden South Indian crepe made from fermented rice and urad dal batter. Best enjoyed with coconut chutney and hot sambar!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Raw Rice
  • 1/4 cup Urad Dal (Split Black Gram)
  • 1/2 tsp Fenugreek Seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed)
  • Oil or Ghee (for cooking)

Instructions

  1. Rinse rice, dal, and fenugreek seeds. Soak together for 4–6 hours.
  2. Grind to a smooth batter using minimal water. The batter should be pourable but not runny.
  3. Ferment overnight or 8–10 hours until bubbly and doubled in volume.
  4. Add salt and mix gently.
  5. Heat a non-stick or cast iron tawa. Pour a ladleful of batter and spread in a spiral motion.
  6. Drizzle oil or ghee around the edges. Cook until crisp and golden. No need to flip.
  7. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
