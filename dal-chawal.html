Dal Chawal Recipe

Dal Chawal

A classic Indian comfort meal made with steaming hot rice and mildly spiced lentil curry (dal). Perfect for lunch or a light dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Toor Dal (or Moong Dal)
  • 2 cups Water
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp Ghee
  • 1 tsp Mustard Seeds
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1 Green Chili (chopped)
  • 5-6 Curry Leaves
  • 1/4 tsp Hing (Asafoetida)
  • 1 cup Cooked Basmati Rice

Instructions

  1. Wash the dal thoroughly and pressure cook with turmeric and water for 3-4 whistles.
  2. Once cooked, mash the dal and add salt. Simmer for 5-10 minutes.
  3. In a separate pan, heat ghee. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  4. Add cumin seeds, chopped chili, curry leaves, and a pinch of hing.
  5. Pour the tempering over the cooked dal and stir well.
  6. Serve hot dal over steamed basmati rice. Enjoy!
