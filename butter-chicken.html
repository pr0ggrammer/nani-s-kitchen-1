Butter Chicken

A rich and creamy North Indian curry made with tender chicken pieces cooked in a buttery tomato-based gravy. Best served with naan or jeera rice.

Ingredients

  • 300g Boneless Chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • 1 Onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 Tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • 2 tbsp Fresh Cream
  • 1 tsp Kasuri Methi (crushed)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh Coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with salt, turmeric, and chili powder. Set aside for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil and butter in a pan. Add onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until raw smell disappears.
  4. Add tomato puree, cook until oil separates, then add remaining spices.
  5. Add chicken pieces and cook until tender and fully cooked through.
  6. Add cream and crushed kasuri methi. Stir well and cook for 2 more minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.
